About Us

Company Profile

Leading footwear brand FootLockRE is founded in Indonesia and has been at the forefront of establishing new shoe industry trends. FootLockRE is devoted to providing the newest and most fashionable shoes for men and has a presence all over the world. Our success is a result of our unrelenting dedication to giving our cherished consumers the best shoes possible.

We are quite proud of the thorough research and development we put forth, always pushing the frontier of creativity to produce cutting-edge ideas. Each pair of shoes is painstakingly handcrafted by our talented team of designers and artisans, with emphasis on outstanding comfort and style.

Company History

FootLockRE was founded with the intention of revolutionizing the footwear market by setting new benchmarks for quality, comfort, and style. The brand has continuously outperformed expectations since its establishment in 1995, giving it a reputation as a global trendsetter in the shoe industry.

FootLockRE has increased the variety of footwear designs it offers, spanning from casual sneakers to formal dress shoes, throughout the years. The company, which is renowned for its workmanship and precise attention to detail, has won praise from both clients and business professionals. With a dedication to breaking down barriers, FootLockRE continues to encourage people to walk with assurance in their excellent shoes.
